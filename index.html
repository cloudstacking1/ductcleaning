What is Air Duct Cleaning?

Air duct cleaning refers to the process of cleaning various components of your HVAC system, including the supply and return air ducts, registers, grilles, diffusers, heat exchangers, cooling coils, drip pans, fan motor, and housing. Over time, dust, pollen, mold, and other debris can accumulate in these components, reducing efficiency and potentially causing health issues.
